How to Order

Kindly complete the order form by specifying your desired quantities of half a dozen and one dozen eggs, along with your preference for either all green, all brown, or a mixed variety. Should you have any special requests, please feel free to leave them in the designated comments section. Following your submission, an invoice will be promptly sent to the email address you've provided. We deeply appreciate your support for our 'Funny Farm' and sincerely hope that you tenjoy your eggs. Thank you for choosing us!
